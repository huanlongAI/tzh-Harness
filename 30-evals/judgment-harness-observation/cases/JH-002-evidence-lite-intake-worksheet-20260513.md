## Intake Boundary

Evidence lite must separate:

- Facts: observable claims with durable source links and dates.
- Assumptions: accepted temporary premises with expiry or recheck triggers.
- Judgments: human decisions or evaluations with named decider and rationale.
- Preferences: owner choices that affect constraints or next actions.

Do not paste secrets, private customer data, token material, credential
prefixes, unreleased customer details, unsigned cross-conversation drafts, AI
outputs, instant chats, or process files.

Use evidence full instead of evidence lite if the signed source asserts unstable
external market, policy, competitor, technical, legal, financial, or medical
facts.
